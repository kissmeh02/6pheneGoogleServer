Why Graphene?

The wonder material reshaping industries. 6phene delivers commercial-grade graphene with atomic precision.

Atomic Strength

A single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a honeycomb lattice provides unparalleled tensile strength, revolutionizing structural integrity in aerospace and defense.

Super Conductivity

Unmatched electrical and thermal conductivity enables faster electronics, superior heat dissipation, and next-gen energy grids with minimal loss.

Chemical Inertness

Highly resistant to chemical corrosion and extreme environments, extending the lifecycle of critical infrastructure and marine vessels significantly.

Our Solutions

Tailored graphene applications for mission-critical industries.

Ballistic Protection

Advanced graphene-composite body armor for military and law enforcement. 40% lighter than Kevlar.

Energy Storage

Graphene-enhanced electrodes for supercapacitors. 5x faster charge rates with extended cycle life.

Smart Concrete

Self-sensing concrete and anti-corrosion coatings for monitoring structural health in real-time.

Thermal Paste

High-conductivity thermal interface material for next-gen CPU/GPU cooling solutions.

Water Filtration

Graphene oxide membranes that filter heavy metals and desalination at the atomic level.

Composite Resins
